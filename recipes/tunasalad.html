Tuna salad

Tuna salad is the most versatile easy healthy meal. Very high in protein and low in carbs. A staple for every broke college student.

Ingredients

  • 4 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
  • 5 large hard boiled eggs
  • 1/2 cup chopped sweet onion
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 3/4 cup mayo
  • 1 1/2 dill pickle relish
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery seed
  • 2 teaspoon honey mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon season salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

  1. Mix tuna, eggs, onion, and celery together in a large bowl.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, relish, honey mustard, celery seed, seasoned salt, and pepper together in a small bowl; pour over tuna mixture and stir gently until all ingredients are coated. Serve immediately, or cover and chill until ready to serve.
